Engineering FAQ

How do you approach system architecture for new SaaS or Fintech projects?
I prioritize scalability and maintainability. I implement component-driven frontends (React) paired with robust Node.js backends, ensuring data integrity through strict validation, PostgreSQL/NoSQL schemas, and clean architectural patterns.
How do you handle security and compliance in regulated domains?
Security is baked into the development lifecycle. I implement JWT/OAuth2 authentication, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), and data encryption (AES-256) specifically tailored for Fintech and Aviation-tech security requirements.
What is your strategy for optimizing performance in data-heavy applications?
I focus on latency reduction and efficient data fetching. This includes implementing Redis caching, optimizing database indexing, and leveraging asynchronous processing for heavy background tasks to keep the UI responsive.
Can you integrate modern stacks with existing legacy systems or APIs?
Yes. Much of my work involves building middleware layers to bridge modern interfaces with complex legacy backends or integrating specialized financial and engineering APIs without compromising system uptime.
What is your workflow for production-grade deployments?
I utilize modern CI/CD pipelines, Docker containerization, and Git. My focus is on automated testing and zero-downtime deployments to ensure stability in user-critical environments.
